Information About heading Tags.

The heading-1 to heading-6 tags are used to define HTML headings.

Heading-1 defines the most important heading. Heading-6 defines the least important heading.

Heading tags are HTML markup used for differentiating headings and sub-headings from the rest of the text in your content.
